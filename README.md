🛡️ Institute of Digital Risk (IDR)

Logo Design & Responsive Homepage

📌 Project Overview

This project presents the branding and homepage design for the Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) — an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on Digital Risk, Cyber Risk, and AI Governance.

The objective of this project is to create a modern brand identity and responsive website that clearly communicates the mission and services of IDR while maintaining a minimal, professional technology-focused design.

🎨 Logo Design

🧠 Design Concept

The logo uses a cube-inspired geometric shape to symbolise:

Structure – representing organised digital systems

Risk Management – analysing and controlling digital threats

Resilience – strong, secure technology infrastructure

The cube form visually reflects data blocks, networks, and layered security, which are central ideas in digital risk and cybersecurity.

🎨 Colour Palette

Colour	Meaning
🟧 Orange	Innovation, energy, and technological progress
⚫ Black	Authority, strength, and professionalism
⚪ White	Simplicity, clarity, and transparency

This palette creates a clean, modern, and professional technology brand identity.

🔤 Typography

A modern sans-serif font is used for the logo text to ensure:

High readability

Professional tech-education appearance

Strong digital compatibility across devices

🔷 Logo Variants

Two logo versions were created to support different branding needs.

1️⃣ Icon Only

Cube-inspired geometric symbol

Used for:

Favicon

Mobile headers

Social media icons

2️⃣ Icon + Text

Cube icon combined with the text:

Institute of Digital Risk

Used for:

Website headers

Official documents

Marketing materials

Both variants are designed to remain clear and legible even at small sizes.

🌐 Homepage Design

The homepage explains:

What IDR is

What services it provides

Who it supports

How organisations or individuals can connect with IDR

🏛️ About IDR

This section explains the institute's purpose.

IDR is an industry-led training and deployment institute that prepares professionals for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles in regulated and high-consequence environments.

The institute bridges the gap between academic research and real-world industry challenges through collaboration with UK universities and industry partners.
